Wake up

Details

    • Set alarm for amount of sleep required
    • Sleep with phone far away
    • Rise from bed and turn off alarm, no snooze
    • Don't go back to sleep

Remarks

    • Remember that last night, when you set the alarm, you deemed this amount of sleep sufficient
    • Last night, you were in a clearer and more deliberate state of mind. This trumps how you feel when you wake up

Fitness

Goals

  • Workout consistently and progress in terms of physique or skills
  • 50 pushups in a row
  • 2 clean chinups in a row
  • 12 minute loop
  • 100 continuous skips

Details

  • 2 day rule: Workout everyday but if there is a lapse then make sure no break is longer than 2 days
  • Experiment: Try working out first thing in morning (preferrably) or after dinner.
  • Chest: do max pushups first, bench press progress on volume and weight, do pushups after as well
  • Back: focus on form, do low weight but remember to progress
  • Every workout: progress towards a 1 minute deadhang, engage core

Remarks

  • It is good to workout with others, because it motivates me and motivates them.
  • I don't need to be the master of everything I do, and can look to others for guidance and help
